# Navigating the Dashboard

Once you're signed in, everything happens inside one screen: a sidebar for navigation, a header for search and quick actions, and the main area showing whichever section you've selected.

## The sidebar

Down the left side (right side in Arabic), you'll find:

| Section | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | Your home screen — live counts of customers, sites, assets, and contracts |
| **Schedule** | Coming soon — see [Demo Mode & What's Coming Next](09-demo-mode-and-whats-next.md) |
| **Dispatch** | Coming soon |
| **Jobs** | Coming soon |
| **Customers** | Your customer, site, and asset registry — see [Customers, Sites & Assets](04-customers-sites-assets.md) |
| **Contracts** | AMC contracts, service lines, and visit plans — see [AMC Contracts & Visit Scheduling](05-amc-contracts-and-visits.md) |
| **Invoices** | ZATCA e-invoicing — see [Invoicing & ZATCA](06-invoicing-and-zatca.md) |
| **Reports** | Coming soon |
| **AI Center** | Coming soon |
| **Support** | FAQ and support tickets — see [Support & Getting Help](08-support-and-help.md) |
| **Settings** | Two-factor authentication — see [Account Security](07-account-security-mfa.md) |

Sections marked "Coming soon" show an honest in-development message rather than fake data — nothing in your real account ever shows fabricated numbers.

## The header bar

- **Search** — a search field at the top-left (visual only today; full cross-record search is on the roadmap).
- **Technician App** — opens a preview of what the technician mobile app will look like. This is a simulation for evaluation purposes; it doesn't read or write your real jobs yet.
- **Dark mode toggle** — the sun/moon icon switches between light and dark themes instantly.
- **Notifications bell** — opens a notifications panel. In your real account, this correctly shows "no notifications" until real notification triggers are built.
- **Language switcher** — the globe icon opens a dropdown to switch between العربية (Arabic) and English at any time. The whole interface — layout direction included — updates immediately.

## Your account card

At the bottom of the sidebar, you'll see your name and role (Owner & Admin, Ops, Accountant, or Technician, depending on your account), plus a sign-out button.

## Company Settings and inviting more teammates later

If you skipped team invites during setup, invite people later the same way described in [Creating Your Account](02-creating-your-account.md) — this surface is being expanded; for now the fastest path is a support ticket from the **Support** tab if you need help getting a teammate added.
