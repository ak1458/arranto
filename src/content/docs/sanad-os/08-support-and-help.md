# Support & Getting Help

## Once you're signed in

Click **Support** in the sidebar. Two things live here:

### FAQ

A set of quick answers to the most common questions — adding a customer, importing a CSV, inviting team members, ZATCA status, resetting your password. Click any question to expand its answer.

### Support tickets

1. Choose a **category**: Technical, Billing, How-to, Feature Request, or Other.
2. Enter a **subject** and a detailed **message**.
3. Click **Submit Ticket**.

Your ticket appears in **My Tickets** below, with a status badge: **Open**, **In Progress**, **Waiting on you**, **Resolved**, or **Closed**. Click a ticket to expand it, read the full thread, and reply — it works like a simple chat thread between you and the support team. Once your issue is handled, an owner or ops-role teammate (or the ticket's original creator) can mark it **Resolved**.

Every business's tickets are completely private to that business — the same data-isolation guarantees that protect your customers and invoices apply here too.

## Before you're signed in

If you're having trouble creating an account, a confirmation email hasn't arrived, or you have a general question before signing up, use the **"Need help? Contact support"** link on the login page — it opens a pre-filled email to arranto support.

## Full documentation

If a documentation link is configured for your deployment, you'll see a **"View full documentation"** link both on the login page and inside the Support tab, pointing to this guide (or wherever arranto has published it).
