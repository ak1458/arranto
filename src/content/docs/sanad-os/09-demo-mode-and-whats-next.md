# Demo Mode & What's Coming Next

SANAD OS is honest about what's real. This page explains the difference between Demo Mode and your real account, and exactly which parts of the product plan are live today versus still being built — so you always know what you're looking at.

## Demo Mode

Click **"Enter SANAD OS Demo Now"** on the login page (no account required) to explore a fully interactive sales/evaluation preview, populated with realistic sample Saudi business data. Every tab is clickable and populated, including features not yet built for real accounts:

- An interactive dispatch board (drag-and-assign technicians)
- Sample jobs with a job-detail view and a "create job" flow
- A technician mobile app preview
- An AI voice-to-work-order simulation
- Sample KPI dashboards and reports

**Everything in Demo Mode is clearly watermarked** with a persistent amber banner, and none of it is ever mistaken for real data — it exists purely so you can evaluate the product's intended experience before committing to it, and it never writes to any real business's records.

## Your real account: what's still being built

These sections currently show an honest "in development" message in your real account rather than any fabricated data:

- **Schedule** — a dedicated calendar view (visits are visible today under each contract's Visit Plan)
- **Dispatch board** — drag-assign technicians to visits
- **Jobs** — full job/work-order lifecycle tracking
- **Reports** — revenue, SLA, and operational reporting
- **AI Center** — voice-note-to-work-order and related AI tooling
- **Quotes** — line-item quotes that convert into jobs or invoices
- **Renewal alerts** — automatic 60/30/7-day AMC expiry warnings
- **Technician mobile app** — an offline-capable field app for technicians (today's header "Technician App" button is a preview simulation only)

We'd rather show you an honest placeholder than a screen full of numbers that aren't real. As each of these ships, this page — and the sidebar itself — will reflect it immediately; there's no separate announcement needed.
